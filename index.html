About Midnight Whispers

In the shadow of midnight, when the world grows still and the veil between realms thins, you’ll hear them — whispers in the dark. Welcome to Midnight Whispers, a podcast where haunted tales, eerie encounters, and witchy folklore come alive. Hosted by Rose Petals and Raven Morningstar, your voices from the other side, each episode takes you deeper into the unknown, echoing with truth, myth, and mystery.

Light a candle, draw the curtains, and listen closely. Some stories stay with you… long after the final whisper fades.
